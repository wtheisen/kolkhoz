## Product Page

Subtitle:
Offline trick-taking card strategy.

Promotional text:
Lead your brigade through tricks, jobs, swaps, and scarce resources in a compact strategic card game.

Description:
Kolkhoz is a compact offline trick-taking card game about planning, sacrifice, and making the most of a difficult hand.

Play cards to win tricks, assign labor to jobs, manage scarce resources, and adapt as the table changes. Each round asks you to balance immediate tempo against long-term survival, with clear local rules and no account required.

Features:
- Offline solo play against built-in opponents
- Trick-taking card play with job assignment and resource pressure
- Multiple rules presets
- Built-in tutorial and rules reference
- Local save/resume
- Reduced motion and clear-text options

## App Review Notes

Kolkhoz is an offline local card game. No account, login, network play, purchases, or tracking are required.

To review:
1. Launch the app.
2. Tap Start Game to begin immediately.
3. Use Rules or How to Play from the lobby for instructions.
4. During a game, use the menu button to return to the lobby or open tutorial options.
