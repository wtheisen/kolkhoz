**Description:**

The plan is ambitious. The harvest will be counted.

Kolkhoz is a strategic trick-taking card game about surviving five increasingly unforgiving years. Win tricks, put captured workers to work, and keep the cards left in your hand hidden in your private plot. Complete the posted jobs—or face requisition when the inspectors arrive.

Download free and play the offline demo. A one-time Full Game Unlock opens the complete game on every supported platform when you sign into the same Kolkhoz account.

Every decision pulls in two directions: the card that wins a trick may be the card you needed to save, and the harvest you assign today may expose your plot tomorrow.

FEATURES

• A complete five-year campaign, from the first plan to the final famine
• Tactical trick-taking with jobs, hidden plots, requisition, and asymmetric special cards
• Several rules presets and optional variants, including the unpredictable Saboteur
• Challenging computer opponents powered by the same deterministic game engine as online play
• Online matches, invitations, comrades, ratings, and progression
• An offline demo that does not require an account
• One permanent Full Game Unlock shared across supported platforms through your Kolkhoz account
• Distinctive hand-crafted pixel art, clear-text options, and light and dark appearances

Highest final plot score wins. Plan carefully: the State remembers a failed harvest.
