# Railway Sleeper-Only Prompt

Built-in image-generation edit using the rejected full-track slice as a
material and print-style reference.

> Convert the reference track slice into a true sleeper-only asset. Remove both
> long vertical steel rails completely and remove all surrounding ballast. Keep
> one horizontal weathered wooden railway sleeper with two compact dark iron tie
> plates and spike hardware at the same rail-gauge positions. The sleeper and
> hardware must be the only subject. Use restrained 1930s Soviet agricultural
> poster lithography: aged printed paper, a limited ochre-brown and charcoal
> palette, graphic silhouettes, and low detail suitable for motion. Use a
> perfectly orthographic top-down view with the sleeper horizontal and centered,
> no perspective, no cast shadow, and generous padding. Place it on a perfectly
> flat solid `#00ff00` chroma-key background with no texture, lighting, gradient,
> or shadow. Exactly one sleeper, exactly two compact fastener groups, no rails,
> no ballast, no gravel, no extra posts, no text, and no watermark.
