# RM40 Y0 terminal depth cards

Flutter exports of the linked RM40 Year 0 components on the Figma `Depth Cards` page.
The five PNGs preserve the accepted 1920 x 800 registration and exact semantic alpha.
The sky remains procedural and uses the shared printed-paper texture.

This is the persistent far backplate for the station-to-camp route. It remains visible
behind every approach card from the beginning of the journey and grows only through the
shared world-space projection. At the Camp stop, Z 8.05, every RM40 card reaches its
accepted master registration in the canonical 1920 x 800 aperture.
