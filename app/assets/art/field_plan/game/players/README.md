# Field-Plan Player Portraits

These 512 x 512 transparent portraits are the first gameplay asset batch for the trick
area:

- `forewoman.png`
- `mechanic.png`
- `agronomist.png`
- `beekeeper.png`

They were generated independently on a flat green background, processed with
`remove_chroma_key.py`, downsampled to 512 x 512, and validated for alpha.

Prompt direction: ordinary early-1930s agricultural workers at human scale; practical
work clothing; calm expressions; utilitarian agricultural-manual and color-lithograph
linework; coal, cream, muted gray-blue, field green, wheat ochre, and restrained brick
red. Avoid heroic poses, invented folk costume, political leaders, flags, medals,
generic political symbols, and military styling.
