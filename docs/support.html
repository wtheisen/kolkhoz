Support

Kolkhoz is an offline trick-taking card game for iPhone. This page covers basic support information for the iOS release.

Getting started

Open the app, choose a rules preset on the lobby screen, then tap Start Game. The Rules and How to Play buttons on the lobby explain the game flow and card actions.

Troubleshooting

Kolkhoz is designed for landscape play. If the interface appears cramped, rotate your device to landscape orientation. You can also use the in-game menu to open the tutorial, switch appearance, or enable clearer text.

Contact

For support, bug reports, or accessibility feedback, email support@williamtheisen.com.
