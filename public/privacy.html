Kolkhoz Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 1, 2026

Overview

Kolkhoz does not collect, sell, rent, or share personal information. The app is designed for offline local play and does not require an account.

Local Data

Kolkhoz may store game progress, preferences, and settings on your device so the app can resume games and remember options such as language, appearance, and text clarity. This data stays on your device and is not transmitted by Kolkhoz.

Analytics and Tracking

Kolkhoz does not use third-party analytics, advertising identifiers, or tracking services.

Children

Kolkhoz does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

This policy may be updated if the app's privacy practices change. The effective date above will be updated when changes are made.

Contact

For privacy questions, email support@williamtheisen.com.
